Sccola Quiz Game

This Quiz Game tests your knowledge of capital cities of the 6 states in the North East Region of Nigeria.

It will also get you familiar with names of cities in the region as all the answers provided as options are real names of cities in the region.

You will be given 5 Questions during each game and you must click an answer before you can move to the next question.

After you click, correct answers are marked with Green background colour while wrong answers are marked with Red background colour

The orders of both the Questions and Answer Options are reshuffle everytime you start a new game

Click on the Button Below to play your game. Good luck!
